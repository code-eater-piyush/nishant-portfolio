Biography

Dr. Nishant Kumar Singh is an Associate Professor in the Mechanical Engineering Department at Harcourt Butler Technical University, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. He earned his Ph.D. in modern machining processes from the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad, India, and his Bachelor of Technology from Delhi Technical University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering), Delhi, India. With 19 years of experience in academia and research, he has established himself as a prominent figure in his field. Dr. Singh has published approximately 120 research papers in high-impact SCI, Scopus, and Web of Science indexed journals. He serves as a reviewer for several reputable journals and is also a member of the editorial team for others. He has mentored numerous M.Tech and Ph.D. students and is the author of ten books published by international houses such as CRC Press, Nova Publishers, River Publishers, and De Gruyter. Furthermore, he is a book series editor for both CRC Press and De Gruyter. Dr. Singh holds over 20 patents and has been recognized by Stanford University as being among the top 2% of scientists worldwide.

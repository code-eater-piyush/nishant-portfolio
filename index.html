Introduction

Hey, I'm Nishant Singh

I am an Associate Professor in the Mechanical Engineering Department at Harcourt Butler Technical University, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. I earned my Ph.D. in modern machining processes from the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad, India, and Bachelor of Technology from Delhi Technical University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering), Delhi, India. With 19 years of experience in academia and research, I have established myself as a prominent figure in my field. I have published approximately 120 research papers in high-impact SCI, Scopus, and Web of Science indexed journals. I serve as a reviewer for several reputable journals and I am also a member of the editorial team for others. I have mentored numerous M.Tech and Ph.D. students and I am also the author of ten books published by international houses such as CRC Press, Nova Publishers, River Publishers, and De Gruyter. Furthermore, I am a book series editor for both CRC Press and De Gruyter. I hold over 20 patents and I have been recognized by Stanford University as being among the top 2% of scientists worldwide.
